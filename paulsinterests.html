Extra-Curricular Activities

Community Advisor

Worked with the Office of Residence Life to support students’ transition to college, fostering a welcoming and supportive environment. Settled roommate conflicts, promoted harmony, respect, and awareness in residential spaces, and organized bi-monthly social programs to build community among students.

Skills: Leadership, Mentorship, Strategic Communication

Bonner Racial Justice Fellow

Developed strategic plans for integrating BIPOC-centered community engagement into programming at the Office of Intercultural Education and the Harward Center. Coordinated anti-racial youth development programs for marginalized youth in Lewiston-Auburn, and led collaborations between BIPOC Bates students and the Tree Street Youth Center to provide opportunities for underserved youth in Maine.

Volunteer Mentor, Aspirations Club

Led career and trade development sessions during Auburn Middle School’s Aspirations Day. Taught high school students in Lewiston-Auburn how to write resumes and cover letters, find job opportunities, conduct mock interviews, and prepare for leadership roles and career exploration.

Class Representative, Campus Safety Advisory Council

Represented the Class of 2027 on student government regarding campus safety. Planned and organized safety awareness programs in collaboration with the Department of Campus Safety and served as a liaison between the student body and the Campus Safety Advisory Council.

Member of the Planning Committee working to shape and refine campus safety policies and educate the Bates community about emergency response protocols.

Secretary, International Students Association

Supported international students’ transition to college life in Maine by organizing social and cultural activities. Planned the annual International Dinner, showcasing diverse traditions and cultures, and facilitated meetings to enhance the inclusivity of the Bates community.
