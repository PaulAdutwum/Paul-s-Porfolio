Extra-Curricular Activities

Community Advisor

Works with the Office of Residence Life to support students’ transition to college, fostering a welcoming and supportive school environment. Mediates roommate conflicts, promotes harmony, respect, and awareness in residential spaces. Organize bi-monthly social programs to promote community building among students.

Skills: Leadership, Conflict Resolution, Strategic Communication

Bonner Racial Justice Fellow

Developed strategic plans for integrating BIPOC-centered community engagement into programming at the Office of Intercultural Education and at Bates College. Coordinated anti-racial youth development programs for youth in Lewiston-Auburn neighborhood. Fasilitated collaborative programs between BIPOC Bates students and the Tree Street Youth Center to provide mentorhsip opportunities for underserved youth in Lewsiton Maine.

Skills: Teamwork, Critical Thinking, Time Management

Volunteer Mentor, Aspirations Club

Lead career and trade development sessions during Auburn Middle School’s Aspirations Day. Educates high school students in Lewiston-Auburn on career development. Leads mock interviews sessions to prepare highschoolers for leadership and career growth.

Skills: Coaching and Mentoring,Leadership

Class Representative, Campus Safety Advisory Council

Represents the Class of 2027 on student government meetings regarding campus safety. Planned and organized campus-wide safety programs, including workshops on emergency response protocols and interactive self-defense demonstrations.

Skills: Advocacy, Policy Development, Public Speaking

Secretary, International Students Association

Supports international students’ transition to college life in Maine by organizing social and cultural activities. Coordinates the annual International Dinner, showcasing diverse traditions and cultures of international students. Liaise with other campus organizations, faculty, and administration to represent the association’s interests.

Skills: Event Planning, Cultural Awareness, Communication

Member, Africana Club

Assisted in organizing cultural workshops, including traditional dance performances, storytelling sessions, and food fairs, providing students with immersive experiences to connect with African cultures.

Skills: Event Planning, Cultural Advocacy, Team Collaboration, Creativity

Student Project Organizer, Physics and Astronomy Club

Organizes and leads weekly meetings to foster collaboration among club members on physics and astronomy projects. Facilitates review sessions and peer-led tutorials to support students in coursework and research, creating a supportive learning environment.

Skills: Collaboration, Academic Support, Project Management

Assistant Coach, Soccer Club

Assists in organizing weekly practice sessions, coaching team members on techniques and strategies to improve gameplay. Coordinates grant applications and oversees logistics for interclub matches to promote teamwork and engagement.

Skills: Team Leadership, Strategy Development, Event Coordination

Outside Hitter, Volleyball Club

Outside hitter for the Bates volleyball.Organizes weekly practice sessions to train new student members. Coordinate all club activities and interclub matches to enhance team cohesion and competitiveness.

Skills: Teamwork, Sportsmanship, Leadership

Student Project Coordinator, Harward Center

Collaborates with Harward Center staff to create and manage community-engaged learning courses and research projects for students. Engages in outreach initiatives with community partners to design issue-based volunteer programs for students. Writes monthly blogs for the Harward Center, highlighting community partnerships and student engagement.

Skills: Community Outreach, Research Coordination, Writing and Communication
